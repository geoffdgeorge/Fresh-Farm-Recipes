Fresh-Farm Recipes

Fresh produce is great.
But what will you make from it?

Farmer's markets. Co-ops. Weekly deliveries right to your door from a CSA. There's a lot of ways to get fresh veggies, fruits, meats, and more these days. The hard part's deciding how to prepare it.

Farm Fresh Recipes takes (most of) the decision-making out of your hands. Just enter any three fresh ingredients, and you'll get five great dishes matched to those ingredients.

You'll also get the source for each recipe, a look at the finished products, and links with info on how to prepare each one. Find a meal that suits your needs today!

Low on supply?

If you've found your recipe but you're low on goods, our handy farmer's market locator will point you to the nearest USDA-registered market to replenish your supply.
