Fresh-Farm Recipes

Fresh produce is great.
But what will you make from it?

Farmer's markets. Co-ops. Weekly deliveries right to your door from a CSA. There's a lot of ways to get fresh veggies, fruits, meats, and more these days. The hard part's deciding how to prepare it.

Farm Fresh Recipes takes (most of) the decision-making out of your hands. Just enter any three fresh ingredients, and you'll get an array of dishes, their ingredients, what they look like, and links with info on how to prepare them.

And, if you're low on goods the handy farmer's market locator below will point you to the nearest places to replenish your supply.
